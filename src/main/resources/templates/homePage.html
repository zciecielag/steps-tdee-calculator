TDEE tracker based on step count


This is a TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) calculator and tracker.
It proposes an alternative way of using activity levels in the calculation.
Instead of using vaguely defined activity categories such as Sedentary, Active etc., it attemps to provide an estimate based on your daily step count and pace.

The equation is simple:
Your TDEE = Your BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate - your energy at rest) + the calories burned from your average daily step count.

WARNING! This calculator and equation are NOT approved by any medical professional and should ONLY be used as a very general reference.
Please remember that your actual TDEE will very likely differ from the one suggested by this calculator.

Please also note that this calculator will not suggest calorie intakes of less than 1200 calories.
